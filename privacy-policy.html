This Privacy Policy explains how LiftEats (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use the LiftEats mobile application (the “App”).

1) What we collect

We collect information in the following categories:

  • Account information: email address and authentication identifiers when you sign up or sign in.
  • Profile & preferences (provided by you): information you enter such as age, height, weight, training goals, and training schedule preferences.
  • Meal and nutrition data: meal entries (including text you type), timestamps, and nutrition estimates generated from your entries (e.g., calories/macros).
  • Fueling insights: training-day markers and fueling scores/insights calculated by the App.
  • App usage and diagnostics: crash/diagnostic data (where enabled) to improve stability and performance.
  • Device & technical data: device type, OS version, app version, and technical identifiers used for app functionality and security.

2) How we use your information

We use your information to:

  • Provide the App’s core functionality (logging meals, computing targets, fueling scores, weekly insights).
  • Personalize recommendations and targets based on your profile and preferences.
  • Maintain security, prevent abuse, and troubleshoot issues.
  • Improve features, performance, and reliability.

3) AI meal logging (Gemini and OpenAI)

Important: Do not submit sensitive personal information (government IDs, financial data, passwords, or medical records) in meal text.

LiftEats includes AI-assisted meal logging. When you submit meal text for AI parsing, we may send that text (and limited related context needed for processing) to third-party AI services, including Google Gemini and OpenAI, to generate structured outputs (e.g., estimated macros).

4) How we share information

We share information only as necessary to operate LiftEats:

  • Infrastructure providers: We use Firebase (Google) for backend services such as authentication and database/storage (as applicable).
  • AI service providers: Meal text submitted for AI parsing may be processed by Gemini and OpenAI as described above.
  • Legal and safety: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect rights, safety, and security.
  • Business transfers: If LiftEats is involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

5) Data retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active and as needed to provide the App. We may retain some information longer when required for legal, security, or operational reasons.

6) Your choices and rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your information. To make a request, contact us at ahmed.ali.tariq@msn.com.

7) Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

8) Children’s privacy

LiftEats is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age required by your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

9) International transfers

If you use the App from outside our operating country, your information may be processed in other countries where our service providers operate.
